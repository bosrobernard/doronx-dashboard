Webhooks

Real-time payment event delivery to your endpoint

Webhooks are callback-based. When a payment is detected on-chain, the gateway pushes the event to DoronX, which then forwards it to your configured endpoint with an idempotency key.

Important Notes

  • Every callback includes an idempotencyKey — always deduplicate on this field to prevent double-billing.
  • Every callback includes a referenceId equal to your paymentIntentId — use this to match the right invoice.
  • The gateway DepositIntent status is never marked PAID — payment status lives entirely within DoronX.
  • Ensure your webhook endpoint is reachable from the DoronX gateway server and returns 2xx promptly.
  • Configure your endpoint URL in your workspace settings.

Delivery endpoint

Configure your webhook endpoint in the workspace settings under Developer → Webhooks. DoronX posts to your endpoint after every state change.

Retry policy

Failed deliveries are retried with exponential backoff. Monitor delivery failures in your Reports section and ensure your endpoint returns 200 promptly.
